Choose Your Room Pricing Plan

Plan 1: Maximum Price with Decreasing Adjustments

This plan starts with the highest price and decreases based on occupancy. The lower the occupancy, the bigger the discount.

Example: If the room price is ₹2000 and occupancy is at 70%, the price will decrease to ₹1800 (10% off).

  • Occupancy > 90%: Full Price (₹2000)
  • Occupancy > 70%: 10% off (₹1800)
  • Occupancy > 50%: 20% off (₹1600)
  • Occupancy > 30%: 30% off (₹1400)
  • Occupancy < 30%: 40% off (₹1200)
Plan 2: Maximum Price with Weekend Increase

This plan applies a price discount based on occupancy and increases the price by 20% on weekends.

Example: If the room price is ₹2000, and occupancy is 60%, the price will decrease to ₹1600 (20% off). If it's the weekend, the price will increase by 20%, making it ₹1920.

  • Occupancy > 90%: Full Price (₹2000)
  • Occupancy > 70%: 10% off (₹1800)
  • Occupancy > 50%: 20% off (₹1600)
  • Occupancy > 30%: 30% off (₹1400)
  • Occupancy < 30%: 40% off (₹1200)
  • Weekend Surcharge: 20% increase on weekends
Plan 3: Smaller Price Decreases with Occupancy

This plan applies smaller price reductions as occupancy increases.

Example: If the room price is ₹2000, and occupancy is 80%, the price will decrease to ₹1900 (5% off).

  • Occupancy > 90%: Full Price (₹2000)
  • Occupancy > 70%: 5% off (₹1900)
  • Occupancy > 50%: 10% off (₹1800)
  • Occupancy > 30%: 15% off (₹1700)
  • Occupancy < 30%: 20% off (₹1600)
Plan 4: Maximum Price with Weekend Surcharge

This plan applies a small discount based on occupancy and adds a 10% surcharge on weekends.

Example: If the room price is ₹2000, and occupancy is 60%, the price will decrease to ₹1800 (10% off). If it's the weekend, the price will increase by 10%, making it ₹1980.

  • Occupancy > 90%: Full Price (₹2000)
  • Occupancy > 70%: 5% off (₹1900)
  • Occupancy > 50%: 10% off (₹1800)
  • Occupancy > 30%: 15% off (₹1700)
  • Occupancy < 30%: 10% off (₹1800)
  • Weekend Surcharge: 10% increase on weekends
